Quiz Funnel Walkthrough

Find Your Perfect Analog Bag

A lead generation quiz built for The Analog Company. It matches visitors to their ideal bag, captures their email, and nurtures them toward a purchase with personalized recommendations.

The Flow

How It Works

1

Visitor Lands on the Quiz Page

A dedicated landing page invites visitors to find their perfect bag. The headline speaks to the core problem: too many bag options, not enough clarity.

2

They Answer 7 Questions

Quick questions about their daily life, carry habits, style priorities, and lifestyle. Card selections, sliders, and toggles keep it engaging. Takes about 2 minutes.

3

Email Capture

After the last question, we ask for their email to send their personalized bag match plus a 10% discount code. No email gate before the quiz starts.

4

Personalized Results

Each visitor sees their score, profile name, and specific product recommendations. Five possible profiles: Organized Multitasker, Expressive Individualist, Practical Minimalist, Life Changer, or Traveler/Adventurer.

5

Automated Email Nurture

14 emails across 5 sequences fire automatically based on their score. Hot leads get purchase nudges. Warm leads get styling inspiration and social proof. Cold leads get brand education.

The Psychology

Why It Works

Solves Decision Paralysis

Your customers are overwhelmed by bag options and unsure which size, style, or type fits their lifestyle. The quiz cuts through that noise and gives them a clear, personalized answer.

Feels Personal, Not Salesy

People love quizzes because they learn something about themselves. Instead of pushing products, you're helping visitors understand their carry style. The recommendation feels earned, not pitched.

Qualifies Leads Automatically

Every answer generates a score. Hot leads (ready to buy) get fast-tracked to purchase. Warm leads get proof and comparisons. Cold leads get educated. No manual sorting needed.

Matches Life Transitions

Many of your buyers are in a life change: new job, new baby, upcoming trip. The quiz catches them at exactly that moment and connects the transition to the right bag.

Distribution

Where to Use It

The quiz works anywhere you can share a link. Here are the highest-impact channels for an e-commerce brand like Analog.

Your Website

Add a "Find Your Bag" button on your homepage, collection pages, and blog posts. Site visitors who take the quiz convert at 3-5x the rate of passive browsers.

Instagram & TikTok

Link in bio, story swipe-ups, and Reels with "take the quiz" CTAs. Quiz content performs well on visual platforms where your bags already shine.

Paid Ads

Use the quiz as your ad destination instead of a product page. "Not sure which bag is right for you? Take our 2-minute quiz." Lower CPA than direct-to-product ads.

Email Signatures & Newsletters

Add a quiz link to your email signature and existing newsletters. Converts passive subscribers into qualified leads with a profile and product match.

Pinterest

Pin the quiz landing page with lifestyle imagery. Pinterest users actively search for bag recommendations and gift ideas. High-intent traffic.

QR Codes

Print on packaging inserts, business cards, or event signage. "Scan to find your perfect Analog bag." Bridges offline to online.
