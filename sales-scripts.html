Warm Lead Script

Opening

"Hey [Name]! Thanks for taking our quiz. Your results show you value [key trait from profile]. I'm curious - what made you take the quiz today? Looking for something specific?"

Discovery Questions

  • "What's not working about your current bag situation?"
  • "Have you tried any solutions before? What didn't work?"
  • "If you could wave a magic wand, what would your ideal everyday bag do?"

Proof Points to Share

  • "We have 1,291 reviews with a 4.83 star average - people really love these bags"
  • "A lot of [profile type] customers tell us [specific testimonial]"
  • "The most common thing we hear is 'I wish I'd found this sooner'"

Nurture Path

"No rush at all! If you want, I can send you some photos of how customers like you are using their bags. Sometimes it helps to see it in action. Would that be helpful?"

Cold Lead Script

Opening

"Hey [Name]! Saw you took our bag quiz - thanks for checking us out. No pressure at all, just wanted to say welcome to the Analog World. If you ever have questions, I'm here!"

Education Focus

  • Share a helpful tip related to their quiz result (bag organization, travel packing, etc.)
  • Ask about their biggest challenge with bags (without pitching)
  • Offer a free resource: "We have a guide on [topic] if you're interested"

Value Demonstration

"Quick tip based on your quiz: [Profile type] customers often struggle with [pain point]. One thing that helps is [actionable tip that doesn't require purchase]. Just thought you might find that useful!"

Long-Game Approach

"When you're ready to explore bags, feel free to reach out. In the meantime, you might enjoy following us on Instagram - we share a lot of organization tips and behind-the-scenes stuff. No pressure though! Welcome to the community."
Objection Response Framework
"It's too expensive" "I totally get it - it's an investment. What I'd say is: most customers tell us they've stopped buying cheap bags that fall apart. At $149 for a bag that does the job of 3, it actually saves money long-term. Plus, free shipping and easy returns if it's not right."
"I need to think about it" "Of course! Take your time. Just so you know, some of our colors and limited editions do sell out. Is there anything specific you're weighing up? Sometimes it helps to talk it through."
